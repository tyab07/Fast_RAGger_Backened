FAST-NUCES (National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences) operates under a Federal Charter granted in July 2000. This ensures all campuses produce degrees of equal legal value.

CFD Campus is located on a 22-acre site, 9km from Faisalabad Motorway Interchange. It supports a Knowledge City philosophy combining isolation with industry proximity.
Key Points:
- Federal Charter = Same degree value
- Self-contained campus
- Industry proximity
